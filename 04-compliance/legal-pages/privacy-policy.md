# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

ILAW Lesson Plan Generator ("we", "our", "us") provides a free online tool that helps teachers create lesson plans following the DepEd ILAW format. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

**We are an independent tool. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Department of Education (DepEd) of the Philippines.**

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

**When you sign in with Google:**
- Email address
- Display name
- Profile picture (optional — we store the URL but do not actively display it)

**When you use the lesson plan generator:**
- Grade level, subject, term, and Learning Competency code selections
- Learning objectives, activities, and assessment content you enter
- This content is processed in your browser and **is not stored on our servers**

**When you subscribe to the Supporter plan:**
- Payment information is handled entirely by **PayPal**. We do not see or store your credit card number, bank account details, or PayPal password.
- We store only: PayPal transaction ID, plan type, and payment status (completed/refunded/failed).

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

We use **Google Analytics** to understand how users interact with our site. Google Analytics collects:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Browser type and version
- Device type and screen resolution
- Approximate geographic location (city-level, not precise address)
- Referring website or search term

**Google Analytics data is anonymized** — we cannot identify individual users from this data. You can learn more about how Google processes this data at [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 2.3 Cookies

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| Session cookie | Maintains your login session (if you sign in) | Session only — deleted when you close your browser |
| Google Analytics (_ga, _gid) | Anonymous user statistics | 2 years / 24 hours |
| Consent cookie (consent_given) | Records your cookie preference | 1 year |

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

**You can control cookies** through the cookie consent banner that appears when you first visit our site. You may also disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.3)
- Passwords: **we do not store passwords** — authentication is handled by Google OAuth
- Payment data: **we do not store payment card details** — all payment processing is handled by PayPal
- Database: Cloudflare D1 with encryption at rest
- Access control: Only the service operator has database access

**No security measure is 100% effective.** We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
