# Refund Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

---

## 1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all paid subscriptions to ILAW Lesson Plan Generator. Free tier users are not affected as no payment is collected.

**We are an independent tool. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Department of Education (DepEd) of the Philippines.**

---

## 2. Supporter Plan ($1.99/month)

### 2.1 Standard Policy

The Supporter plan is a recurring monthly subscription. Because the subscription grants immediate access to features (ad-free experience, batch generation, ZIP export, LC Code auto-expand), refunds are handled as follows:

- **If you experience a technical issue** that prevents you from using the Supporter features, contact us within **7 days** of the payment date. We will investigate and, if the issue is on our side, issue a full refund.
- **Change of mind / no longer need the service:** We evaluate these on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us and we will review your situation within 3 business days.

### 2.2 How to Request a Refund

1. Email us at **support@[domain].com**
2. Include:
   - The email address used to sign in
   - The PayPal transaction ID (found in your PayPal receipt)
   - A brief description of the issue
3. We will respond within **3 business days**

### 2.3 Refund Processing

- Approved refunds are processed through PayPal back to your original payment method
- Refund processing time: 5-10 business days (depending on your bank or PayPal)
- We may deduct PayPal processing fees (approximately 3-5%) from the refund amount if the refund is issued outside of our standard policy

## 3. Pro Plan (Future)

When the Pro plan becomes available:

- **7-day money-back guarantee** for new subscriptions

- Partial refunds for subsequent months on a case-by-case basis

## 4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

- Free tier usage (no payment was made)
- Subscription periods already used (partial refunds may apply on a case-by-case basis)
- PayPal transaction fees (when the issue is not on our side)

---

## 5. Cancellation and Billing

- You may cancel your Supporter subscription at any time
- Cancellation stops future billing — you retain access until the end of the current billing period
- After the current period ends, your account reverts to Free tier
- No refunds for partial billing periods unless noted above

---

## 6. Dispute Resolution

If you are unsatisfied with our refund decision:

1. Reply to our email within 14 days explaining why you disagree
2. We will escalate to a senior team member for review
3. If still unresolved, you may file a dispute through PayPal's Resolution Center
