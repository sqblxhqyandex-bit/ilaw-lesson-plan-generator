### § AI Generation Credits

**1. Free Tier.** The Recipe Generator is free to use without limit. No account or payment required.

**2. AI-Powered Generation.** AI-powered lesson plan generation is a paid feature. You must:
   - Sign in with a Google account
   - Have sufficient AI credits in your account

**3. Credit Packs.** AI credits are sold in one-time packs:

   | Pack | Price (USD) | Credits | Valid for |
   |---|---|---|---|
   | AI Starter | $0.99 | 10 | 12 months |
   | AI Regular | $2.99 | 50 | 12 months |
   | AI Teacher Pack | $5.99 | 150 | 12 months |

   Credits are valid for 12 months from the purchase date. Unused credits expire after 12 months.

**4. Free Trial.** New users receive 3 free AI credits upon signing in. Free trial credits expire 30 days after registration if unused. One free trial per Google account.

**5. Credit Usage.** Each AI generation request consumes 1 credit regardless of output length. Failed generations (server error, timeout) do not consume credits. Exporting or downloading an already-generated plan does not consume credits.

**6. No Subscription.** Credit packs are one-time purchases. No recurring charges. No auto-renewal.

**7. Refunds.** Refunds for unused credit packs are available within 7 days of purchase. To request a refund, contact hello@ilawlessonplan.net. Refunds for partially used packs are at our discretion. Promotional or free trial credits are not eligible for refund.

**8. Abuse.** Automated, bulk, or scripted generation using paid credits is prohibited. We reserve the right to revoke credits and terminate accounts engaged in abuse.

### § No Affiliation

ILAW Lesson Plan Generator is an independent tool. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Department of Education (DepEd).

### § Disclaimer

AI-generated lesson plans are drafts. Teachers must review and adapt all generated content for their specific classroom context, learner needs, and alignment with official curriculum guides.
