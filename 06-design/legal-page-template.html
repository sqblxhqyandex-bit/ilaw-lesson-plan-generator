Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

ILAW Lesson Plan Generator ("we", "our", "us") provides a free online tool that helps teachers create lesson plans following the DepEd ILAW format. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

We are an independent tool. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Department of Education (DepEd) of the Philippines.

2. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google: Email address, display name, and profile picture URL.

When you use the lesson plan generator: Grade level, subject, term, and Learning Competency code selections — processed in your browser, not stored on our servers.

When you subscribe to the Supporter plan: Payment is handled entirely by PayPal. We only store transaction ID, plan type, and payment status.

3. Cookies

We use a session cookie (maintains login), Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _gid — anonymous usage stats), and a consent cookie (records your preference).

4. Data Storage and Transfer

User data is stored on Cloudflare D1 (United States). If you access from the Philippines, your data is transferred to the US. By using this service, you consent to this transfer.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your data by contacting support@[domain].com. If you are in the Philippines, you have rights under RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012).
