📋 What is a Budget of Work?

A Budget of Work (BoW) is an official DepEd resource that maps out the learning competencies to be covered each term. It helps teachers plan their lessons for the school year by showing which competencies to teach, in what order, and how much time to allocate. The Three-Term BoW is aligned with the MATATAG K-10 curriculum under DO 009, s. 2026.
