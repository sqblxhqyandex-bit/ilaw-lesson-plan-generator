What Is Included in the Free ILAW Template?

Each template includes the four required sections of an ILAW lesson plan, giving you a complete structure that aligns with DepEd guidelines:

  • Intentions: Space for learning competency code from the MATATAG CG and 2-3 measurable objectives.
  • Learning Experiences: Activities, teaching strategies, materials, and time allocation.
  • Assessment: Formative and summative assessment ideas aligned with your objectives.
  • Ways Forward: Remediation, enrichment, and next lesson connections.

How to Fill Out the ILAW Template

  1. Open the template in Word, PowerPoint, or your preferred editor.
  2. Enter your school name, grade level, subject, section, and learning competency.
  3. Write clear, measurable objectives under Intentions.
  4. Describe your activities, materials, and strategies under Learning Experiences.
  5. Add formative and summative assessment tools under Assessment.
  6. Plan remediation, enrichment, and next steps under Ways Forward.
