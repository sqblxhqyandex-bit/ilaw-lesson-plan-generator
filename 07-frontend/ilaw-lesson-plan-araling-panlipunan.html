Sample AP ILAW Lesson Plan

Grade 7 AP — Importance of Studying History

  • I: Students will explain the importance of studying history in understanding the present.
  • L: Brainstorming, timeline activity, primary source analysis, and class discussion on current events.
  • A: Short written reflection connecting a past event to a present issue.
  • W: Remediation with guided questions; enrichment with research on a local historical figure.

Teaching Tips for AP

  • Use local examples and community stories to make history relevant to students' lives.
  • Incorporate primary sources such as letters, photos, artifacts, and interviews with elders.
  • Connect past events to present-day issues for deeper understanding and critical thinking.
  • Use maps, timelines, and graphic organizers to help students visualize historical change.
  • Encourage multiple perspectives when discussing historical events and figures.
