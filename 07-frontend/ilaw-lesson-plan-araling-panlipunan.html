Generate complete ILAW-format lesson plans for Araling Panlipunan (AP) under the DepEd MATATAG K-10 curriculum. Our free generator covers Philippine history, geography, civics, culture, and economics for all grade levels.

AP Topics by Grade Level

  • Grades 1-3: Family, community, school, Filipino values, basic map skills, local heroes
  • Grades 4-6: Philippine regions, pre-colonial history, Spanish period, American period, local government
  • Grades 7-8: Asian history, world history, Philippine constitution, human rights, globalization
  • Grades 9-10: Economics, entrepreneurship, contemporary issues, sustainable development, civic engagement

Sample AP LC Codes

  • AP4KPP-IIIe-5: Natatalakay ang kahalagahan ng pagkakaisa at pagtutulungan sa pamilya at komunidad
  • AP5PKP-IVa-1: Naipapaliwanag ang kontribusyon ng mga Pilipino sa pagbuo ng bansa
  • AP7KPK-Ia-1: Naipaliwanag ang kahalagahan ng pag-aaral ng kasaysayan
  • AP10KPE-Ia-1: Naipapaliwanag ang kahalagahan ng ekonomiya sa pang-araw-araw na buhay

Teaching Tips for AP

  • Use local examples and community stories to make history relevant
  • Incorporate primary sources: letters, photos, artifacts, interviews with elders
  • Connect past events to present-day issues for deeper understanding
  • Use maps, timelines, and graphic organizers extensively
  • Encourage critical thinking about multiple perspectives in historical events
