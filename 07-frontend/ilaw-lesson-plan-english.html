Why Use an ILAW Format for English?

The ILAW format helps English teachers move beyond grammar drills and focus on meaningful communication. By clearly stating intentions and designing authentic assessments, you can better support literacy development across all grade levels.

Assessment Ideas for English

English assessments can include reading response journals, writing rubrics, oral presentations, spelling tests, comprehension quizzes, peer editing checklists, and performance tasks. The generator suggests assessments that match your selected topic and grade level.
