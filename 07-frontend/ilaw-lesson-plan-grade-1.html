Grade 1 Subjects Covered

Grade 1 Learning Characteristics

Grade 1 learners are typically 6-7 years old. They learn best through play, hands-on activities, visual aids, and repetition. Our ILAW lesson plans incorporate:

  • Short attention-span friendly activities (10-15 minutes per segment)
  • Visual and kinesthetic learning strategies
  • Games and interactive elements
  • Simple language and clear instructions
  • Positive reinforcement and encouragement
