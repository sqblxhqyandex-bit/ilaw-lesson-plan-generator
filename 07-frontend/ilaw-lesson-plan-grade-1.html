Grade 1 Subjects Covered

  • English: Beginning reading, phonics, letter recognition, simple sentences, listening comprehension, and oral language.
  • Filipino: Alpabeto, pagbasa, payak na pangungusap, pakikinig, at pagsasalita.
  • Mathematics: Number sense, counting, basic addition and subtraction, shapes, patterns, and measurement.
  • Science: Living things, senses, weather, plants, animals, and basic observations.
  • MAPEH: Simple songs, basic movements, drawing, and health habits.
  • Araling Panlipunan: Family, community, school, and basic Filipino values.
  • ESP: Good manners, respect, sharing, honesty, and love for family.

Grade 1 Learning Characteristics

Grade 1 learners are typically 6-7 years old. They learn best through play, hands-on activities, visual aids, and repetition. Our ILAW lesson plans incorporate:

  • Short attention-span friendly activities (10-15 minutes per segment)
  • Visual and kinesthetic learning strategies
  • Games and interactive elements
  • Simple language and clear instructions
  • Positive reinforcement and encouragement

Sample Grade 1 ILAW Lesson Plan

Grade 1 English — Letter B Recognition

  • I: Students will identify the uppercase and lowercase forms of the letter "B."
  • L: Letter song, tracing activity, picture matching, and group chant.
  • A: Teacher observation and oral questioning.
  • W: Extra tracing sheets for students who need practice; advanced students find "B" words around the classroom.

Teaching Tips for Grade 1

Keep activities short, concrete, and engaging. Use songs, movement, and visual aids. Provide frequent praise and break complex tasks into small steps. Always connect lessons to students' daily experiences and familiar objects.
