ILAW Lesson Plan for Grade 4 — MATATAG Curriculum Aligned

Grade 4 is part of the MATATAG curriculum rollout. All Grade 4 teachers are required to use the ILAW format for lesson planning.

Grade 4 Subjects Under MATATAG

Grade 4 covers: English, Math, Science, Filipino, Araling Panlipunan, MAPEH, Values Education, and GMRC (Good Manners and Right Conduct). Each subject requires a separate ILAW lesson plan.

How to Adapt ILAW for Grade 4

Grade 4 learners are transitioning from primary to intermediate level. Learning Experiences should balance concrete activities with introduction to abstract concepts. Assessment should include both formative checks and performance tasks.
