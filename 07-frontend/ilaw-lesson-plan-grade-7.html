Grade 7 Subjects Covered

  • English: 21st Century Literature, Philippine Literature, grammar, composition, oral communication
  • Filipino: Panitikan ng Pilipinas, gramatika, pagsulat, pagbasa, pag-unawa

Grade 7 Learning Characteristics

Grade 7 learners are typically 12-13 years old, transitioning from elementary to secondary education. They need:

  • Structured routines with increasing independence
  • Connections between subjects and real-world applications
  • Opportunities for critical thinking and analysis
  • Collaborative learning with peer interaction
  • Clear expectations and rubrics for assessment
  • Support for social-emotional development during adolescence
