MAPEH Components Covered

  • Music: Rhythm and melody, musical symbols, Philippine folk songs, instrumental techniques, music history, and music appreciation.
  • Arts: Elements of art, color theory, drawing and painting, printmaking, sculpture, and Philippine visual arts.
  • Physical Education: Motor skills, games and sports, physical fitness, dance, outdoor recreation, and team building.
  • Health: Nutrition, personal health, disease prevention, substance abuse prevention, mental health, and safety.

How ILAW Works for MAPEH

MAPEH lessons often involve hands-on performance and creative expression. The ILAW format helps you state clear learning intentions, design active learning experiences, plan authentic assessments, and plan next steps for students with different skill levels.

Sample MAPEH ILAW Lesson Plan

Grade 6 Music — Philippine Folk Songs

  • I: Students will identify the characteristics of Philippine folk songs.
  • L: Listening activity, song analysis, and group singing.
  • A: Students complete a comparison chart of two folk songs.
  • W: Advanced students research regional variations; struggling students review with audio guides.

Assessment Ideas for MAPEH

Safety in MAPEH Lessons

Physical activities and art projects require safety reminders. Always include warm-up and cool-down routines for PE, proper use of materials for Arts, and hygiene reminders for Health lessons.
