ILAW Lesson Plan Samples by Subject & Grade Level

Browse these sample ILAW lesson plans to understand how the format works for different subjects and grade levels. Each sample follows the I-L-A-W structure required by DepEd Order 16 s.2026.

ILAW Lesson Plan Sample — Grade 4 English

Subject: English | Grade: 4 | Topic: Identifying Main Idea

  • I — Intentions: Students will identify the main idea of a grade-level informational text.
  • L — Learning Experiences: Read-aloud, think-pair-share, graphic organizer completion, and small group discussion.
  • A — Assessment: Exit ticket where students write the main idea of a short paragraph.
  • W — Ways Forward: Students who need support will practice with shorter sentences. Advanced students will identify supporting details.

ILAW Lesson Plan Sample — Grade 7 Science

Subject: Science | Grade: 7 | Topic: Scientific Method

  • I — Intentions: Students will describe the steps of the scientific method.
  • L — Learning Experiences: Video introduction, lab activity, group presentation, and class discussion.
  • A — Assessment: Lab report rubric evaluating hypothesis, procedure, and conclusion.
  • W — Ways Forward: Struggling students review vocabulary. Advanced students design an extension experiment.

ILAW Lesson Plan Sample — Grade 9 Math

Subject: Mathematics | Grade: 9 | Topic: Linear Equations

  • I — Intentions: Students will solve linear equations in one variable.
  • L — Learning Experiences: Guided examples, pair problem-solving, and real-life word problems.
  • A — Assessment: Short quiz with five equations and one word problem.
  • W — Ways Forward: Remediation for errors in sign handling; enrichment with systems of equations preview.
