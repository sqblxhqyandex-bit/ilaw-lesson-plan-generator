How to Use the ILAW Template

  1. Click the download button above to get your Word or PPT template.
  2. Open the file in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, PowerPoint, or Google Slides.
  3. Fill in your school name, grade level, subject, and Learning Competency code.
  4. Write your content in each I-L-A-W section.
  5. Review against DepEd Order 16 s.2026 guidelines.
  6. Print, submit, or present your lesson plan.

Why Use an ILAW Lesson Plan Template?

Many Filipino teachers are still adjusting from the old DLL/DLP format to the new ILAW format. A template removes the guesswork by giving you the correct sections, spacing, and headings already formatted. You can focus on writing strong lesson content instead of worrying about document layout.
