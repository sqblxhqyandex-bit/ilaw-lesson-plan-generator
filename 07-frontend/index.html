ILAW FRAMEWORK

The Four Checkpoints Behind Every ILAW Lesson Plan

Every plan you create follows the same I-L-A-W structure, making it easier to align with the MATATAG curriculum and DO 16 s.2026.

I

Intention

Clarify learning objectives, competencies, success criteria, and what learners should achieve by the end of the lesson.

L

Learning Experience

Design teacher facilitation, learner activities, group work, and resources that develop target competencies step by step.

A

Assessment

Plan formative checks, evidence of learning, and performance tasks that measure understanding throughout the lesson.

W

Ways Forward

Prepare remediation for struggling learners, enrichment for advanced students, and reflection activities to close the lesson.
