Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

Free Recipe use: The lesson plan is generated in your browser. We may collect anonymous usage events such as grade level, subject, and page path for product analytics.

Google sign-in: When you sign in, we collect your email address, display name, Google account ID, and profile picture URL.

AI generation: We process the lesson topic, LC code, objectives, grade level, subject, selected design principles, and AI output for generation and audit logs.

Payments: PayPal processes your payment. We store transaction IDs, SKU, payment status, amount, and credit balance. We do not store full payment card details.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare for hosting and D1 database, Google OAuth for sign-in, PayPal for payments, DeepSeek for AI generation, and consent-gated analytics tools such as Google Analytics, Ahrefs, or Plausible.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We use a session cookie to keep you signed in. Analytics scripts load only after consent through the cookie banner, where implemented. Analytics are used to understand site performance and teacher usage patterns.

4. AI Provider Processing

When you use AI generation, your prompt data is sent to the configured AI provider to create a lesson draft. Do not enter student names, private student records, or sensitive personal information.

5. Data Retention

Account, payment, credit, and AI generation audit records are retained as needed for service operation, fraud prevention, refunds, and legal compliance. You may request deletion where applicable.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as necessary with service providers: Cloudflare, Google, PayPal, DeepSeek, and analytics providers after consent.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your data by contacting support@ilawlessonplan.net. If you are in the Philippines, you may have rights under RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012).
