Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Free Recipe Generator

The Recipe generator is free. No refund applies to free usage.

2. AI Credit Packs

You may request a refund within 7 days of purchase if fewer than 10% of the purchased credits have been used.

  • AI Starter (30 credits): eligible only if 3 or fewer credits used.
  • AI Pro (120 credits): eligible only if 12 or fewer credits used.
  • AI Teacher Pack (350 credits): eligible only if 35 or fewer credits used.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Free trial credits, expired credits, accounts suspended for abuse, and credit packs substantially used beyond the thresholds above are not eligible for refund.

4. Failed AI Generations

If an AI generation fails because of a server error or AI provider error, the credit should be automatically refunded. If this does not happen, contact support with the approximate time of the failed request.

5. How to Request a Refund

Email support@ilawlessonplan.net with your Google account email, PayPal transaction ID, and the reason for refund. We aim to respond within 3 business days.

6. Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed through PayPal. PayPal or your bank may take additional time to return funds to your payment method.
